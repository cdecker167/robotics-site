History

Club History

The Pickerington North Robotics club was founded in 2017 as the Pickerington North "Tech Club," started by the members pictured from 2017-2018 down below. It has evolved over time, and is still growing in size and prowess. We have competed in competitions across Ohio, and continue to hone our skills in the wide world of Vex Robotics.


Pictured above is the mounted head of the club's first robot, Rome (circa. 2017)
