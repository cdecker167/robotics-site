When: Every Tuesday, 3:00-4:30

Where: PHSN Room 109 (Mr. Good's room)


Every Tuesday, Pickerington North students meet to build, code and drive robots. Whether it's to compete in the Vex Competition, or just for fun, all who attend are greeted with a fun and creative atmosphere that fosters ingenuity. Not sure if you want to join? Come visit us to see what we're up to!


Teachers



kevin_good@plsd.us

Room: 109

Mr. Good teaches computer science, engineering, and mathematics at North. We build and code all robots in his room, which is where the club meets once a week.



herman_moultrie@plsd.us

Room: 304

Mr. Moultrie teaches computer science and video courses at North, along with running the North News Network. The Vex field resides in his classroom.


Feel free to email Mr. Good or Mr. Moultrie if you're interested in joining, or join our discord and talk to one of us. We'd be happy to answer any questions you have.
